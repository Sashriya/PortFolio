Biography

I’m a passionate software developer dedicated to creating clean, user-friendly digital experiences that make a difference. With a strong foundation in front-end and back-end development, I focus on building intuitive platforms that are both functional and visually appealing.
My recent work includes developing a cloth donation platform that bridges the gap between donors and communities in need—an initiative close to my heart, aimed at using tech for social good.
Outside of coding, I have a deep appreciation for the arts and storytelling. Whether it's capturing moments through photography, designing impactful visuals with Adobe InDesign, or diving into the world of creative writing, I find joy in expression and detail. Currently, I'm exploring projects that combine my technical skills with meaningful impact—building platforms for education, sustainability, and community connection.
When I’m not coding or creating, you’ll likely find me behind a camera lens, sketching design ideas, or writing short stories.
